OUR BUSINESS

Sean's Pizza and burger Restaurant (“SPBR”) core business is the development, operation and franchising of its quick-service restaurant brands. It offers a wide variety of affordable and delicious dishes and great tasting food prepared to satisfy customers of all ages and from all walks of life. Food quality, service, price-value relationship, store location and ambiance, and efficient operations continue to be critical elements of My Restaurant’s success in the quick-service restaurant industry.
